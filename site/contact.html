Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum stay?
Our minimum stay varies by property. Most short-term rentals have a 2-night minimum, while mid-term and long-term options start at 30 days. Group Living has a 1-month minimum with month-to-month flexibility.
What's included in the rental price?
All properties come fully furnished with premium linens, towels, kitchen essentials, Wi-Fi, and smart TV access. For Group Living, utilities and cleaning are also included in one flat monthly rate.
How do I book a property?
You can book directly through us for the best rates with no third-party service fees. Simply fill out the inquiry form above or email bookings@travellir.com. We'll confirm availability and send you a secure booking link within 24 hours.
Are pets allowed?
Pet policies vary by property. Many of our properties are pet-friendly with prior approval. Please mention any pets in your inquiry and we'll match you with the right property.
What is Group Living?
Group Living by Travellir offers private, furnished rooms in shared residences in Marlinton, WV. It's ideal for remote workers, ski season visitors, and outdoor enthusiasts who want a hassle-free, community-oriented living experience near Snowshoe Mountain with flexible month-to-month leases.
